Introduction About ReBrain

Have you read the book 'PS, I love You' by 'Cecelia Ahern'? It is the story of Holly, who lost her husband Gerry at 30 years of age and felt she had lost her identity and her sense of direction. The letters helped Holly find her feet, her voice, herself again, and her husband, though gone, was able to continue to guide her on her journey through grief, give her strength to heal again and reach a point of hope.
What happens is that the rush of being perfect, of changing ourselves for others, of taking responsibilities, we get tangled up in such a way that our true selves get lost somewhere in the darkness. The fear, the anxiety, the pain, the hurt....we just try to lock it up, scared to face it...forgeting the fact that by doing so we are haming ourselves the most. here a a small initiative by which you get to acknowledge yourself. A small step to put yourselves first before others. take some time out, love yourselves and automatically you will improve your mental health. so lets refresh our brains.

21 Days Challenge

Have you ever thought of picking up a good habit, but never got around to doing it? Perhaps you tried to do so for 1-2 days, but lacked the discipline to continue on. Perhaps you are overwhelmed by the effort needed to develop the new habit and never got around to doing it. Introducing the 21-Day Habit Trial Program. :) This is a self-initiated program where you stick to a certain habit for 21 days, every day. While it can be used to cultivate new habits, you can use it to test out any new activity, such as waking up early, quitting soda, sticking to a new diet, making new friends, meditating, going to work early, doing an act of kindness, etc. The 21-day trial is not limited to good habits â€” you can use it to break bad habits. Do you have any bad habits, such as spending too much time on social media, putting off work to the last minute, binge eating, sleeping late, smoking, or biting your nails? You can breaking these bad habits with the 21-day trial as well.
